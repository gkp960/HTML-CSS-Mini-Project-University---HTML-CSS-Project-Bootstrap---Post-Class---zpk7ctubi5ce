Explore the mouthwatering flavours of #Rajasthan

The cuisine of Rajasthan is primarily vegetarian and offers a fabulous variety of mouthwatering dishes. The spice content is quite high in comparison to other Indian cuisines, but the food is absolutely delicious. Rajasthanis use ghee for cooking most of the dishes.

Rajasthani food is well known for its spicy curries and delicious sweets.
